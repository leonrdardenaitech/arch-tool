# WaveSync: User Instructions 📋

## Setup
1.  **Connect to Wi-Fi:** Ensure your phone and your Smart TV (Roku or Chromecast) are on the same local network.
2.  **Open WaveSync:** Launch the app and wait for the **Connection Hub** to populate.
3.  **Target Selection:** Select your device (e.g., "Living Room Roku") from the dropdown.
4.  **Activate Sensor:** Tap the **"Big Switch"** (the massive listening button). It will turn from grey to active blue.

## Using Gestures (The "Jedi" Manual)
The sensor is located near the earpiece/front camera of your phone. Use smooth, broad hand movements about 2-5 inches above the screen.

*   **👋 1 Quick Wave (Swipe):** 
    *   *Action:* Rewind 15 Seconds.
    *   *Use Case:* "Wait, what did that recipe just say?"
*   **👋👋 2 Quick Waves (Double Swipe):** 
    *   *Action:* Play / Pause.
    *   *Use Case:* "Doorbell is ringing, stop the music!"
*   **✋ Steady Hover (Hold for 2 Seconds):** 
    *   *Action:* Mute Audio.
    *   *Use Case:* "Need to talk to the client without reaching for the volume knob."

## Accessibility Options
*   **Haptic Feedback:** The phone will vibrate briefly when a gesture is recognized.
*   **Visual Confirmation:** The "Listening..." button pulse will flash when a command is fired.
*   **Custom Thresholds:** (Coming Soon) Adjust the sensitivity of the sensor to accommodate different lighting or hand sizes.
